About Us

Seventy years bringing floral arrangements
to the city of Limerick

Mill flowers have been in existence since the 1950's. Three generations of our family have worked in the family business, artistically hand-arranging flowers for every occasion. We provide services from everyday bouquets, birthday and holiday gift baskets, funeral baskets, special occasion arrangements, and more! No matter what occasion, Mill Flowers will put every ounce of love, dedication and personalization to every piece that is created by us.
